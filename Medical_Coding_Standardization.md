# Case Study: Clinical Narrative to Structured Data (ICD-10 & DRG)

As a Clinical Documentation Specialist, I map narrative patient data into standardized codes to ensure data integrity for healthcare algorithms.

| Clinical Narrative (Input) | ICD-10 Code | DRG Category | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Patient with severe headache, blurred vision, and BP 165/110 mmHg at 32 weeks gestation. | *O14.13* (Severe pre-eclampsia, third trimester) | *774* (Abortion w/o D&C) or *768* (Hypertension) | Identification of "Severe Features" (neurological symptoms + BP). |
| 45yo male with polyuria, polydipsia, and HbA1c of 8.5%. | *E11.65* (Type 2 diabetes mellitus with hyperglycemia) | *638* (Diabetes w CC) | Mapping metabolic markers to specific chronic condition codes. |
| Acute chest pain radiating to left arm; elevated Troponin levels. | *I21.9* (Acute myocardial infarction, unspecified) | *282* (Acute Myocardial Infarction, Discharged Alive w/o CC/MCC) | Transitioning acute symptoms into definitive diagnostic coding. |
